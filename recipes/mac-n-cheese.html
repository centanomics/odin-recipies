Mac and Cheese

Description

Quick and easy side dish. Very cheesy. Very good.

Ingredients

  • Kraft mac and cheese box
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil 8oz of water
  2. Put the Kraft pasta in the pot and boil for 10 mins
  3. Drain pasta and then put it back in the empty pot
  4. Mix in butter, milk, and kraft cheese powder and mix
  5. Enjoy
