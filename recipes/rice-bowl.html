Cent's Rice Bowl

Description

Cent's rice bowl is a cheap and easy meal that you can customize to your liking. It will usually contain rice, some form of meat, and some form of vegetables

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ground pork
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Your favorite blend of spices
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Put one serving of rice into a rice cooker
  2. While the rice is cooking start browing the ground pork, mixing in the spices while the ground pork is cooking
  3. Once the ground pork is close to done you can heat up the mixed vegetables in the microwave
  4. Once the rice is done, add your ground pork and mixed vegetables and mix it all together
  5. If you want you can add cheese as well
