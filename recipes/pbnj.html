Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Whether you're looking for a quick snack or just need a furfulling lunch, a peanut butter and jelly sandwich will give you what you want.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Grape Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Once bread is done, take it out and put peanut butter on one slice
  3. Put the grape jelly on the other slice
  4. Put the two slices together
  5. If you want, slice the bread in half
